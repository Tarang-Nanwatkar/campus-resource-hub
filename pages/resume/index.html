Quick Tips for Success

1. The 6-Second Rule

Recruiters scan quickly. Put your most impressive skills in the top third of the page.

2. Quantify Results

Instead of "Lead a team," use "Lead a team of 5 to increase sales by 20%."

3. No Pictures

Unless you're in a specific creative region, avoid photos to ensure ATS compatibility.
